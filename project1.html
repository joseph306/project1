Social engineering

summary

Social engineering is an attempt to manage social changes in the future and the development and behavior of a society. Social engineering is also the use of deception to manipulate into divulging confidential or personal information. Common types include: phishing, pretexting, and baiting

who started this?

Jacques Marken is the person who developed the term social engineering. He started using the term social engineering in the 1890. He uses this to improve the social world.

why does this exist?

Social engineering exists because it is simpler to exploit human trust than it is to hack passwords. People have a need to help others this makes social engineering attacks easy. criminals can send an email with a malicious link or even impersonate other people in order to infiltrate businesses to obtain information that can be used to take over.
